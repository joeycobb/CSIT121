My Favorite Color

red, orange, yellow, GREEN, followed by blue...

I am someone who loves rainbows and so I try not to discriminate among the many colors, but there is one that objectively stands above the rest. That is green. I am well aware that green is a very broad term, ranging from deep forest green to pale mint. I enjoy pretty much all of the varieties that the color has to offer. I use this color as inspiration for many things, like interior decoration, character and fashion design in my art, and school supplies. Many shades pf green have become a big part of my style and expression, so I am not looking forward to revamping if my favorite color ever changes!
